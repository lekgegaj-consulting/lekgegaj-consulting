## 4. LOGO SYSTEM

### Logo Variations

1. **Full Logo** (Primary)
   - Horizontal oval badge with gold border
   - Company name in Playfair Display
   - Tagline below
   - Globe icon integrated
   - Minimum width: 240px
   - Use on light backgrounds

2. **Compact Logo** (Secondary)
   - Horizontal format without tagline
   - Minimum width: 180px
   - Use in headers, navigation

3. **Icon Mark** (Tertiary)
   - Globe symbol only
   - Size: 48px minimum
   - Use as favicon, app icon

4. **Monochrome Logo**
   - Gold version on navy background
   - Navy version on cream background
   - For restricted color environments

### Logo Spacing & Clearance

- Minimum clear space: 20px on all sides
- Never overlap with other elements
- Never rotate, skew, or distort
- Never change color without approval
- Never remove or alter any element

### Logo Placement

- **Header:** Compact logo, left-aligned, 60px height
- **Footer:** Compact logo, left-aligned, 50px height
- **Favicon:** Icon mark, 32x32px
- **Hero Section:** Full logo, centered, 200px width

## 10. ANIMATION & INTERACTION

### Transition Timing

- **Fast:** 0.15s (micro-interactions)
- **Standard:** 0.3s (button hover, fade)
- **Slow:** 0.5s (page transitions, major animations)
- **Easing:** ease-in-out (smooth, professional)

### Hover States

- **Buttons:** 0.3s color/shadow transition
- **Links:** 0.2s color transition, underline appears
- **Cards:** 0.3s shadow/lift transition
- **Icons:** 0.2s color transition

### Scroll Animations

- **Fade-in:** Elements fade in as they enter viewport
- **Slide-up:** Content slides up with fade
- **Scale:** Cards scale slightly on scroll
- **Parallax:** Subtle background movement (hero section)

### Loading States

- **Skeleton:** Navy #0F1B3C with gold accent pulse
- **Spinner:** Gold rotating circle, 2px stroke
- **Progress:** Gold bar on navy background

## 11. ACCESSIBILITY

### Color Contrast

- Text on background: Minimum 4.5:1 ratio (WCAG AA)
- Navy #0F1B3C on Cream #F8F7F4: 12.5:1 ✓
- Gold #C9A961 on Navy #0F1B3C: 4.8:1 ✓

### Typography

- Minimum font size: 14px body text
- Line height: 1.5 minimum for readability
- Letter spacing: Adequate for dyslexia-friendly reading

### Interactive Elements

- Minimum touch target: 44x44px
- Focus states: Visible 2px gold outline
- Keyboard navigation: Full support
- Screen reader: Semantic HTML, ARIA labels

## 12. BRAND VOICE & TONE

### Voice Characteristics

- **Professional:** Authoritative, knowledgeable, expert
- **Approachable:** Clear, accessible, not condescending
- **Trustworthy:** Honest, transparent, reliable
- **Sophisticated:** Refined language, premium positioning
- **International:** English and Albanian, culturally aware

### Tone Guidelines

- **Formal:** Legal services, official communications
- **Conversational:** Business consulting, guidance
- **Empathetic:** Client concerns, support
- **Confident:** Expertise, capabilities, results

### Language Principles

- Use active voice
- Avoid legal jargon when possible
- Be specific and concrete
- Use "we" to build connection
- Emphasize client benefits
